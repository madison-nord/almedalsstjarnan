# Store Description (English)

Star events during Almedalsveckan and export your personal schedule as an ICS calendar file.

## Description

Almedalsstjärnan makes it easy to plan your week at Almedalsveckan, Sweden's largest political festival held annually in Visby, Gotland. Browse the official programme at almedalsveckan.info and star the events you want to attend — right in your browser.

**Features:**

- ⭐ Star events directly on the almedalsveckan.info programme page
- 📋 View all your starred events in a dedicated overview
- 📅 Export your schedule as an ICS file for any calendar app
- 🔍 Sort and filter your starred events
- 🔒 All data stored locally on your device — no account required

Perfect for attendees of Almedalsveckan who want to keep track of their personal schedule without creating accounts or sharing data with third parties.
